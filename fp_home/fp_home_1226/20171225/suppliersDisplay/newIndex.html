Promexico and Freshport Exchange Meeting

Promexico and Freshport Exchange Meeting is successfully held on 7th Nov. PROMEXICO SHANGHA and Mexican producers visit the Huizhan Market and the cold stores. Mexcican producers show great interest to cooperate soon. Importers also value this face-to-face meeting very much, with more than 15 importers attended, during the meeting 8 mexican producers introduce their business one by one. Most of them have 2-5 years experience in exporting, but mainly to US, Canada and EU, so they want to seek more chinese partners to promote mexican avocados, berries business through Freshport. This meeting provides opportunities for mexican avocado, berry producer and chinese importers to exchange information about the market to start and establish long-term cooperation soon. Freshport bridges these seller and buyers together to promote fruits industry communication, easier and faster.
