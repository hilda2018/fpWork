ABOUT US

Online Information & Service Platform for Imported Fruits Industry

FreshPort is a subsidiary of Oheng Group--- a service provider dedicated for imported fruit business for over 15 years.

Now FreshPort & Oheng are providing the importers and exporters in fresh fruit business with services including trading agency, customs clearance, international logistics, supply chain financial, trading promotion, wholesale market management and consignment taxation management.

We have more than 100 long-term stable customers which were located mostly in the main imported fruit wholesale market covering all the mainland Chinese market.

In 2016, the total amount which all transacted through Oheng has reached 600 million USD between these customers and their 300 global suppliers. Meanwhile, more than 12000 containers and 20000MT air shipment of imported fruits has been imported to those customers. On the other hand, we have invested the new Haiguangxing wholesale market in Jiaxing which is planned to open in the third quarter of 2018, based on our customer feedback we are confident that this wholesale market will soon becoming one of the main wholesale markets in China.
