  Background for Consignment Twice Taxation  

There is no settlement price for imported fruit until sales completed (Settlement= Sales price-commission-taxation-sales expenses). So the customs imported taxation is based on price evaluation which is always different from settlement price, so the legal risk for importers and overtax for exporters always exist. To satisfy clients’ requirement and secure long-term healthy development for the whole industry, under the supervision and guidance of Shanghai Customs, FreshPort has set up an open and innovative third-party consignment taxation platform to help importers pay consignment taxation by law and actual returns

  Core functions for Consignment Twice Taxation  

Forwarders key in basic information for each shipment;
Importers provide online sales report;
Importers provide online sales report;
FreshPort generate twice taxation report automatically to be verified by Customs;
FreshPort assist Customs to dynamically supervise the sales price and expenses.

  Why Consignment Twice Taxation  

  • Easy to operate
    Exporters only need to do online confirmation
  • Avoid legal risk
    No more legal risk for importers
